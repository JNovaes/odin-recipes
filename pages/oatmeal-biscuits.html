Ann's Oatmeal Biscuits

This is a family recipe that for years was kept on a scrap of paper tucked away in an old recipe book in our kitchen. The recipe came from an old neighbour of hers on the Kilcorig Road and has been improved on by my mother, Clare. Growing up, this was my favourite food to bake.

Ingredients

  • 170g Butter
  • 85g Castor Sugar
  • 85g Plain Flour
  • 170g Organic Jumbo Oats
  • 1/2tsp Baking Soda

Method

  1. Cream the butter and sugar until smooth.
  2. Add and mix the plain flour, oats and baking soda.
  3. With wet hands, roll into small 'golfballs' and flatten.
  4. Bake on a sheet at 150 degrees (130 fan oven) until golden.
